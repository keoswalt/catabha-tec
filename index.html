About

Mark Andrews spent over 30 years as the general manager for Roller Equipment Manufacturing Company, Inc. In 2021, he started Catabha-Tec LLC. Since then, he has helped over a dozen companies from Houston to Tokyo ensure the highest performance from their rubber roller equipment.

Mark is based in Kansas City, Missouri where he lives with his wife, Theresa, and enjoys spending time with his kids and grandkids, hunting, and volunteering in his community.
